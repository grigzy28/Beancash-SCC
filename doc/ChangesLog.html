    • Changes in Ver. 1.1.2.2RC (25 December 2017)

    Rebrand from BitBean to Bean Cash. In September 2017 the name Bitbean was officially published as a service trademark of a United States Corporation known as 1on1 Development LLC. Bean Core was not involved in the original naming of BitBean, the digital currency and network, and as such, was unaware that another entity was using the name. In August 2017, Bean Core's executive board reached a consensus and published a RFC to the community regarding a proposal to re-brand. The community agreed and the name was changed from BitBean to Bean Cash.
